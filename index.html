Our Story

At Amma Organic Home Foods, we believe in preserving tradition through taste. Our pickles are handmade with organic ingredients and age-old recipes passed down from generations. Experience the warmth of home in every bite.

Every jar is prepared with the same care and love that Amma puts into her cooking, ensuring authentic flavors that remind you of childhood memories and family gatherings.

We source our ingredients directly from local farmers who share our commitment to organic and sustainable practices, guaranteeing the highest quality products for your family.
